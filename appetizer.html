Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta is an Italian appetizer or antipasto consisting of grilled bread slices rubbed with garlic and topped with a variety of toppings, such as chopped tomatoes, basil, and olive oil. Other toppings may include mozzarella cheese, cured meats, vegetables, and herbs..

Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese salad is a simple Italian salad that typically consists of sliced fresh tomatoes, sliced fresh mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. The salad is often drizzled with olive oil and seasoned with salt and pepper.

Chicken Wings

Description

Chicken wings are a popular appetizer made from the chicken wing drumettes and flats, which are typically fried, baked, or grilled until crispy and golden brown. Chicken wings can be served plain or coated in a variety of sauces, such as buffalo sauce, barbecue sauce, honey garlic sauce, or teriyaki sauce.

Hummus

Description

Hummus is a type of mashed dish originated around Middle East. It's an Arabic name. In the West, it is sometimes eaten as an appetizer with crackers or as a snack. The dish which includes lemon juice, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, olive oil and garlic is a Middle East cuisine that is usually eaten as a dip together with pita bread.

Shrimp Cocktail

Description

Shrimp cocktail is a classic appetizer that typically consists of chilled, cooked shrimp that are served with a tangy, tomato-based cocktail sauce. The cocktail sauce is often made with ketchup, horseradish, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and hot sauce, and can be adjusted to taste with additional spices and seasonings.

Description
