Potato Soup

Ingredients:

4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
1 cup milk
1/2 cup heavy cream
2 tablespoons butter
Salt and pepper to taste
Optional toppings: shredded cheddar cheese, chopped bacon, sliced green onions

Instructions:

In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.

Add the diced potatoes and chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the potatoes are soft and easily pierced with a fork.

Using an immersion blender or transferring the soup to a blender, blend until the soup is smooth.

Add the milk and heavy cream, and stir to combine. Simmer the soup for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Taste the soup and season with salt and pepper as desired.

Ladle the soup into bowls and top with shredded cheddar cheese, chopped bacon, and sliced green onions if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
