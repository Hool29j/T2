Vegetarian Salad

Description

Vegetarian salad is a dish that excludes meat or fish and includes a variety of ingredients such as leafy greens, vegetables, fruits, nuts, seeds, and grains. Dressings made from olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, honey, mustard, and herbs are often used to add flavor. It's a nutritious and customizable meal option that's perfect for those following a vegetarian or vegan diet, and can be served as a main course or side dish.

Description

Vegetarian stir-fry is a quick and easy dish made by stir-frying a variety of vegetables in a wok or skillet with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. It's a healthy and versatile meal that's perfect for a busy weeknight.
