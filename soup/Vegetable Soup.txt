Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 medium carrots, chopped
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 medium sweet potato, peeled and chopped
1 can diced tomatoes (14.5 ounces)
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried oregano
4 cups vegetable broth
1 cup water
1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
2 cups chopped kale or spinach
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they begin to soften.

Add the carrots, celery, and sweet potato to the pot and continue to sauté for another 5 minutes.

Stir in the diced tomatoes, thyme, and oregano and let simmer for 5 minutes.

Add the vegetable broth, water, and kidney beans to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Stir in the chopped kale or spinach and let cook for another 2-3 minutes until wilted.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Serve hot and enjoy!
